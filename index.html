Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are your protocols for COVID-19?

A: Your safety and the safety of our team members are our top priority during all stages of the project. That is why our team members are regularly tested before and during projects, as well as wearing protective face coverings at all times and maintaining physical distance.

Q: What happens if someone tests positive for COVID-19?

A: All involved team members are notified and the project will be temporarily put on hold (usually about 2 days) while team members get tested. Team members who do not test positive for COVID-19 will resume the project the day after receiving test results..

Q: What happens if I, or someone in my household tests positive for COVID-19?

A: Safety is our top priority. In the event you or someone in your household tests positive for COVID-19, all work on the project will be put on hold until all affected personnel test negative for COVID-19.

For more information about COVID-19 please see fda.gov for more information.
